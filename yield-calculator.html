What Is Buy To Let Yield?

Buy to let yield measures the return on investment for a rental property. It is calculated by dividing the annual rental income by the property purchase price and multiplying by 100.

Gross Yield

Gross yield is calculated before expenses and gives a quick indication of the rental performance of a property investment.

Net Yield

Net yield includes annual costs such as maintenance, insurance, letting agent fees and void periods. This provides a more realistic view of profitability.

Typical UK Rental Yields

Most UK buy-to-let properties produce gross yields between 4% and 8%, although this varies significantly depending on location and property type.
